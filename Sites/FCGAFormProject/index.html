Fulton County Government Authorization for Payroll Deduction/Health Savings Account Contribution

This form is for you to authorize your employer to deduct money from your paychecks throughout 2025 and deposit it into your Health Savings Account (HSA) on a pretax basis. To begin payroll deductions, you must be enrolled in the Anthem HSA Plan. If you are enrolled in the Anthem HMO Plan, the Anthem POS Plan, or the Kaiser HMO Plan, you cannot contribute to this account. HSA payroll deductions continue through December 31, 2025. To make HSA contributions for the following plan year, you must make a new election. Money from your pay is deposited into your HSA account at MetLife after each payroll run.

Return this form to employeebenefits@fultoncountyga.gov by the appropriate deadline. Please keep a copy for your records. Once your documentation is received and validated, your HSA account will be opened, and you will receive a welcome communication and debit card. Please be sure to log into your online account and activate your debit card.
